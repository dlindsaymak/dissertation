DEEP CTN EFA (Pilot 3 SONA Win/Spr23)

Jan 19, 2024 11 min read EFA

This is the third pilot study testing the factor structure of the new CTN scale. Pilot data was collected using SONA from February - April 2023

Sample Descriptives

Total sample size was N = 485 . The mean age of sample was 20.37(2.38). Age range: 18 - 42

Political Ideology was scored on a 7-point Likert scale with 1 = Most Conservative and 7 = Most Liberal. The mean political ideology was 5.02(1.14), showing that this sample is skewing slightly more left-leaning.

SES was scored using the SES Ladder, with 1 being the worst off, and 10 being the best off. Mean sample was 5.72(1.48).
