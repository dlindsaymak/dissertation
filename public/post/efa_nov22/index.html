DEEP CTN EFA (Pilot 1 Prolific Nov22)

Jan 26, 2024 14 min read EFA

This is the first pilot study testing the factor structure of the new CTN scale. Pilot data was collected using Prolific on November 8, 2022.

Sample Descriptives

Total sample size was N = 575 . The mean age of sample was 29.61(6.11). Age range: 18 - 44

Political Ideology was scored on a 7-point Likert scale with 1 = Most Conservative and 7 = Most Liberal. The mean political ideology was 5.18(1.55), showing that this sample is skewing slightly more left-leaning.
